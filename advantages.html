Advantages of using Drupal over other frameworks
Cost: Drupal's cost cannot be beat at literally nothing; It is completely free.
Ease of use: Drupal is extremely easy to install.
Dependability: Drupal is extremely reliable and robust
Customization: The Drupal Core can be extended with a wide range of add-ons.
Scalablility: Drupal can scale up easily as a business grows.
